Store Policies




 

Payment Methods Accepted
We accept payments via Credit Card (American Express, Discover, MasterCard or Visa), PayPal, and U.S. Personal Checks or Money Order.

Reliable Service! Satisfaction Guaranteed! 

Bookmarc's is a professional bookseller and we conduct a thorough inspection of every item we offer for sale. We strive to accurately determine the authenticity of every item. We also make every effort to accurately describe any defects, blemishes, or other characteristics which reduce an item's quality from 'As New' or 'Fine'. Our descriptions never intentionally conceal material defects, overstate a book's condition, nor are intended to mislead buyers in any way. A full refund will be made to the purchaser for any misrepresented item if it is returned to us in the same condition as shipped out. We never knowingly purchase or sell stolen materials, and at all times offer our active and complete cooperation with authorities for the recovery of stolen items. We accept full responsibility for damage to, or loss of, items in transit.

Returns and Refunds 

Bookmarc's has a flexible refund policy with a 100% money back guarantee on books returned within 20 days of the date they are mailed to you.


Shipping 

We use the United States Postal Service. Standard shipping is via media mail within the United States, but airmail shipping is also available. We also mail books worldwide by surface mail and by airmail.


Special shipping or delivery needs? 

We will use alternate delivery services at your request. Please contact us regarding any general questions you might have about your special shipping or delivery requirements as well as for quotes for any type of domestic or international shipping. Also, please see our shipping charges page for more details.


Additional Services 

All books with dustjackets that have a sales value of $10 or more have mylar protection added. Giftwrap and gift card services are also available on request.
